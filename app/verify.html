FERPA
Always verify

Keep these in mind when testing and during review.

  • Teacher-owned items must not appear to other teachers.
  • Students must not see other students’ private content.
  • Public-facing moderation (avatars, news) belongs in the shared queue; mission review belongs to the owning teacher.
  • Mission review is only for the teacher who owns the mission (My Classroom).
